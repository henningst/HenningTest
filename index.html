Life at Talentech

Talentech has set out to become the largest and most innovative HR tech company of its kind. The product suite today spans the entire talent pipeline from recruitment, to onboarding and talent management with our products Talent Recruiter, ReachMee, Webcruiter, Talmundo, Weekli, irecommend and ontame.

We know that great HR makes a difference and we want to be a part of shaping the future with the vision that all companies should know that Talent Comes First. To reach our goals we need you! Talentech as a workplace is characterized by a lot of great and engaged colleges that really believe in what we do.

Our recruitment process

1

Submit Your Application

Start your journey by applying through our career portal; upload your CV and share a bit about yourself.

2

Initial Screening

We will review your application to assess the alignment between your skills and the job requirements.

3

Interview Process

Engage with us through one or more interviews to discuss your experiences, skills, and aspirations.

4

Assessment

Depending on the role, you may be invited to complete assignments or assessments to showcase your capabilities.

5

Offer & Onboarding

If it's a match, we'll extend an offer. Once accepted, we'll guide you through every step of your onboarding journey.
